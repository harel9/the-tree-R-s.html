one of the most interesting and importante issues that i theach
 is ecology and how to save the environment. As humans, we consume a lot of resources
 from nature to benefit from them. Without noticing, every day we dump a very large
 amount of waste, which is mostly made of plastic, which harms animals, pollutes the environment
 , and mostly pollutes the air we breathe.On this site, I want to show you about
 these three ways to preserve the environment in which we live.

To reduce the problem, you can use the following three simple but creative ways:

  1. Reduce - Wise consumption of products can reduce the amount of our use
    of substances that may pollute the environment.
    For example: you should use lunch boxes instead of using plastic bags every day and throwing them in the trash.
    This will reduce consumption in plastic bags.
    Also, there are many ways in which fuel and electricity consumption can be reduced to keep air cleaner.



  2. Reuse - In this way we take a product that we have finished using and use it again for another purpose.
    For example: There are schools like the school where I teach that encourages reuse of plastic products
    and use them again to decorate the school space.
    Also, there are ways to reuse water. There are technological systems that know
    how to filter some of the water we use at home, to use for irrigation



  3. Recycle - Recycling is a process in which waste is dismantled and
    returned to a state of raw material, which will be used to produce new products.
    Nowadays there are special bins for recycling glass, plastic, paper, metals, electronic waste etc.
